Top 3 Ranked Movies of 2024

A Short Critique by Judith.

2024 seems to be a good year for movies. Although, I have not watched a lot of movies this year, but all of them seemed good to watch, and I would likely recommend you to watch as well. I will rank from top 3 down to my top 1 movie choice.

3. Carry On

Earning the 3rd spot this year. I liked how this thriller/mystery movie made me think how the lead male would be able to save his girlfried while saving the people from the airport as well.

2. It Ends With Us

Here is my number two choice. This follows the story of a woman who grew up with an abusive father and tries to find love, only to find out that her lover is also an abusive man. Great story.
